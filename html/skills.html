STAGES OF WORK

Discussion of the project
Let's discuss our vision of the project: what problems we should solve with the help of the site, its functionality and your design preferences. We will agree on the timing and cost of the work
Creating a website prototype
I will propose the structure of the site and create a prototype based on all the information received from you, analysis of competitors and target audience
Design development
I will select several stylistic references and draw several design options for the main page. Once approved, I will complete the design of the entire site
Moving the site to Tilda
I will work on all the pages of the site on Tilda, set up functionality and animation. I will also adapt your site so that it is convenient to view from all devices

FAQs

How does payment work?

Payment for designer services can occur in various ways, including payment per project, hourly payment. Payments can be made via bank transfer, electronic payment systems or other methods as agreed

How much does it cost to create a website?

The cost of creating a website can vary widely depending on the complexity of the project, the features and functionality required, and the expertise of the designer or agency. For more details, see the corresponding section

How long will the work take?

The timeline for creating a website can also vary depending on the complexity of the project and the availability of the designer or agency. A simple basic website may take a few weeks to complete, while a more complex and custom-designed website could take several months. It's important to discuss your timeline expectations with the designer or agency to ensure that they can meet your needs within your desired timeframe

Do you create presentations, checklists and other materials?

I can work with clients to understand their needs and preferences, and then use design software to create visually appealing and effective materials. If you need assistance with creating specific materials, feel free to reach out to me for help
